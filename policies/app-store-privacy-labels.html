Data Sharing/Sale

Sale: No. We do not sell personal data.
Sharing: Shared with service providers for functionality (hosting, analytics, maps, ads).

Tracking

If using advertising, Apple’s AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) prompt may be required on iOS. Android users can limit ad personalization in device settings.

User Controls

  • Delete account/data by emailing info@midasensemble.com.
  • Toggle location permissions (precise/background) in OS settings.
  • Cookie banner for website controls (see Cookie Policy).
