Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Midas Ensemble Technologies LLC allows users to request deletion of their account and personal data.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email info@midasensemble.com with the subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. From the email address associated with your account, include: your full name and (if applicable) your username.
  3. We may ask you to verify your identity.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information (name, email, phone)
  • Content you submitted (subject to shared content still visible to others)
  • Device identifiers associated with your account

What We May Retain

  • Data necessary to comply with legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforce our agreements
  • Aggregated or anonymized data that cannot reasonably identify you

Timeline

We endeavor to process verified requests within 30 days, subject to lawful extensions.
